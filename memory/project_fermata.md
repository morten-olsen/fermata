**Fermata** is a GPL3 React Native (Expo) music player. Solo project.

Core identity: rediscovering the forgotten art and joy of listening to music. Inspired by a lineage of devices — vinyl (commitment to an album), Walkman (personal curation), iPod (focused navigation) — that each made listening an intentional act. Streaming killed that. Fermata brings it back.

**Why:** Modern music apps turned listening into a utility. Fermata is for people who want the joy back.

**How to apply:** Every decision should pass the test: "does this honor the act of listening?" No algorithms, no interruptions, no social features. Prefer album-focused browsing, linear drill-down navigation, and mix tapes as first-class personal curation. The music is always front and center.
