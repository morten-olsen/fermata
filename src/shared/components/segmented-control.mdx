## Design rationale

The segmented control embodies Fermata's iPod-inspired philosophy: **one path forward, one choice at a time**. Instead of tabs, sidebars, or dropdowns, a simple horizontal control lets you flip between related views.

## Where it's used

The segmented control appears on the **Library screen** to switch between Albums, Artists, and Tracks views. This is the iPod pattern: instead of parallel navigation, you choose a lens to view your library through.

## Usage

```tsx
const [selected, setSelected] = useState(0);

<SegmentedControl
  segments={["Albums", "Artists", "Tracks"]}
  selectedIndex={selected}
  onSelect={setSelected}
/>
```
