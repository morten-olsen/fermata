## The belief

Listening to music is one of the most joyful things a person can do — and the tools
people use to listen should honor that.

The name comes from the musical notation 𝄐 — a held note. A deliberate pause. It means:
*stop here, hold this, give it your attention*.

## The test

Every feature, every screen, every interaction should pass one question:

<blockquote style={{
  borderLeft: '3px solid #D4A0FF',
  paddingLeft: 20,
  margin: '24px 0',
  fontStyle: 'italic',
  fontSize: 18,
  color: '#E8E8ED',
}}>
  Does this help someone sit down and joyfully listen to music?
</blockquote>

If yes, build it. If no, don't. If maybe, wait until the answer is clear.
