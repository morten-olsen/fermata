# Now Playing

<p style={{ color: '#9898A3', fontSize: 16, marginBottom: 24 }}>
  The heart of Fermata вҖ” a vinyl-inspired full-screen player and a compact mini player that lives above the tab bar. Both adapt their palette to the currently playing album art.
</p>

<Canvas of={FullStories.Playing} />

## Design rationale

<div style={{ display: 'grid', gridTemplateColumns: '1fr 1fr', gap: 16, margin: '24px 0' }}>
  <div style={{ padding: 16, backgroundColor: '#141416', borderRadius: 12 }}>
    <p style={{ fontWeight: 600, color: '#D4A0FF', marginBottom: 4 }}>Album color is the room</p>
    <p style={{ fontSize: 13, color: '#9898A3' }}>The full player extracts primary and secondary colors from the album art and paints a three-layer gradient wash across the entire screen. The music doesn't just play вҖ” it fills the space.</p>
  </div>
  <div style={{ padding: 16, backgroundColor: '#141416', borderRadius: 12 }}>
    <p style={{ fontWeight: 600, color: '#D4A0FF', marginBottom: 4 }}>Vinyl disc, not a rectangle</p>
    <p style={{ fontSize: 13, color: '#9898A3' }}>Artwork is clipped to a circle with a CD-style center hole, rotating continuously during playback. The rotation decelerates on pause вҖ” a physical, tactile metaphor for the music stopping.</p>
  </div>
  <div style={{ padding: 16, backgroundColor: '#141416', borderRadius: 12 }}>
    <p style={{ fontWeight: 600, color: '#D4A0FF', marginBottom: 4 }}>Waveform progress</p>
    <p style={{ fontSize: 13, color: '#9898A3' }}>A seeded waveform visualization replaces a flat progress bar. The shape is deterministic per track вҖ” the same song always draws the same waveform. Interactive: drag to seek.</p>
  </div>
  <div style={{ padding: 16, backgroundColor: '#141416', borderRadius: 12 }}>
    <p style={{ fontWeight: 600, color: '#D4A0FF', marginBottom: 4 }}>Breath animation</p>
    <p style={{ fontSize: 13, color: '#9898A3' }}>On play/pause, the artwork container gently scales вҖ” a subtle "inhale" on play, "exhale" on pause. Just enough to feel alive without distracting.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Full player

The full-screen Now Playing view is a destination. It's designed to reward focused listening.

### Playing state
<Canvas of={FullStories.Playing} />
Vinyl rotates, waveform fills with the album-derived tint, "Next up" pill shows the queued track.

### Paused state
<Canvas of={FullStories.Paused} />
Vinyl decelerates to a stop. Play icon replaces pause. Everything else holds still.

### Warm tones
<Canvas of={FullStories.WarmTones} />
Different album art produces an entirely different atmosphere вҖ” warm ambers and deep reds here vs cool blues above. Same component, album-driven personality.

### No artwork
<Canvas of={FullStories.NoArtwork} />
Falls back to a neutral palette with a musical notes icon. Still functional, still dignified.

The mini player is a persistent strip above the tab bar вҖ” always visible when music is playing.
