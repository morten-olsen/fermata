# MediaCard

<p style={{ color: '#9898A3', fontSize: 16, marginBottom: 24 }}>
  A compound card component for browsing media. Ships with Album, Show, and Book presets, plus composable sub-components for custom layouts.
</p>

<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.Album} />

## Design rationale

<div style={{ display: 'grid', gridTemplateColumns: '1fr 1fr', gap: 16, margin: '24px 0' }}>
  <div style={{ padding: 16, backgroundColor: '#141416', borderRadius: 12 }}>
    <p style={{ fontWeight: 600, color: '#D4A0FF', marginBottom: 4 }}>Album art is the interface</p>
    <p style={{ fontSize: 13, color: '#9898A3' }}>Large artwork dominates the card. Title and subtitle are secondary, single-line, and truncated. The visual identity of the media should be recognizable at a glance in a grid.</p>
  </div>
  <div style={{ padding: 16, backgroundColor: '#141416', borderRadius: 12 }}>
    <p style={{ fontWeight: 600, color: '#D4A0FF', marginBottom: 4 }}>Badges communicate state</p>
    <p style={{ fontSize: 13, color: '#9898A3' }}>A download badge confirms offline availability. A progress badge shows audiobook completion. A new-episode indicator draws attention. Badges overlay the artwork corner so they never compete with text.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Presets

Presets are pre-composed configurations of the sub-components, tailored to each media type. Use them for standard library grids.

### Album

Square artwork, disc fallback icon, optional download badge. Subtitle shows artist and year.

<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.Album} />
<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.AlbumDownloaded} />

### Show (podcast)

Square artwork, mic fallback icon, episode count badge. New-episode indicator appears as an accent dot on the top-right corner of the artwork.

<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.Show} />
<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.ShowWithNew} />

### Book (audiobook)

Portrait artwork (3:4 ratio), book fallback icon. Progress badge takes priority over download badge when both could apply.

<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.Book} />
<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.BookWithProgress} />

## Custom composition

When presets don't fit, compose directly with the sub-components. The root `MediaCard` wraps children in a pressable container; nest `Artwork`, `Title`, `Subtitle`, and any badge as needed.

<Canvas of={MediaCardStories.CustomComposition} />

```tsx
<MediaCard onPress={handlePress}>
  <MediaCard.Artwork
    uri={artworkUri}
    fallbackIcon="musical-notes"
    badge={<MediaCard.CountBadge icon="musical-notes" count={24} />}
  />
  <MediaCard.Title>Late Night Mix</MediaCard.Title>
  <MediaCard.Subtitle>24 tracks</MediaCard.Subtitle>
</MediaCard>
```
