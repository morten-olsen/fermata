## Design rationale

The album card is the visual heart of Fermata. The design system's first principle is **"the music is the interface"** — and album art is how that manifests. Large, prominent artwork makes browsing feel like flipping through a record collection.

## Artwork resolution

Album cards don't store artwork URLs. The database stores an `artworkSourceItemId`, and `resolveArtworkUrl()` generates the URL at render time based on the source adapter. This means:

- Artwork URLs can change (e.g., new auth token) without re-syncing
- Different sizes can be requested for different contexts
- The same album from multiple sources resolves independently
